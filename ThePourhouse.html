The Pourhouse

Specialty drink- “Iced Caramel Macchiato”
The Pourhouse’s caramel macchiato is mediocre. The caramel and vanilla flavors were just not that great. I was very disappointed by this speciality drink, as I expected better from a place with such a great reputation. Personally, I enjoyed the muffin more than the drink itself.

The Pourhouse’s cold brews/black coffee aren’t memorable. The coffee is good, but not great. They are reasonably priced for what you get. What sets the Pourhouse apart when it comes to straight coffee is its option of a bottomless cup. It’s free refills until you can’t drink anymore. It’s perfect for when you have to study somewhere for hours at a time. Their menu also features a good number of lunch items, which are both healthy and tasty.

Atmosphere:
The Pourhouse is the most unique in terms of identity. They recently expanded the store and the number of seating is extensive. One room is flooded by light, and the other has seating from the windows to a backroom. On the wall and chalkboard, you see world maps, which are more than just pretty wall decor. The Pourhouse’s tips are donated to different third-world countries in need. While you get to drink some delicious coffee, you also get to help another person!
