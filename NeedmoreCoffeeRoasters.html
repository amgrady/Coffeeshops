Needmore Coffee Roasters

Specialty drink- “Honduras, 18 Rabbit”
Though this drink was black coffee, and not what we expected, I was really impressed by it. I could taste each individual flavor in the roast, especially the caramel and apple. The presentation of the drink was especially unique because they gave us the coffee in a mug, but the extra was given to us in a chemical flask. We watched them measure everything and prepare it elaborately, which is no doubt part of the reason Needmore had the best black coffee I had in Bloomington.

Atmosphere:
The area is very laid back and open. It makes a perfect study spot since it’s not too busy, it’s quiet and has a comfy couch and tables by windows. Needmore is a great example of an authentic, unique coffee shop. They get high quality roasts from around the world, and include coffee bags on the walls from these locations, giving the place a worldly vibe. It’s the place for you if you appreciate different roasts of black coffee and quiet environments. If you prefer louder, busy coffee places and sugary dessert drinks, this wouldn’t be your favorite place to go.
