Soma

Soma’s drip coffee/cold brews are flavorful and well-priced. There’s a wide variety of brews to choose from, and the coffee is high quality. The standard coffee drinks are all made well, and they have a large selection of unique specialty drinks as well as several types of loose-leaf teas, freshly-squeezed juices, and fruit smoothies. Whether you like black coffee or caramel macchiatos, Soma has something for you!

Specialty Drink- “Cherry Garcia”
This speciality drink features a sweet combination of dark chocolate and cherry, but the chocolate taste seems to overpower the cherry flavor. It’s more reminiscent of a hot chocolate than a specialty coffee or latte. If you prefer drinks that are more dessert-like than coffee-like, this drink might be something to try.

Atmosphere:
Soma’s atmosphere is cozy and comfortable. It’s split into two rooms, the main room and back room; behind the back room is a small record store. While the other coffee shops seem to have a theme, Soma’s style is a very random selection of antiques and vintage items. Its bathroom features a cardboard-cutout of Elvis and graffiti coats the wall, while the back room is covered in world maps and bookshelves. Though this is a popular spot in Bloomington, it’s not an ideal place to study. There’s not much natural lighting, as compared to other local shops, and the wi-fi is very slow. Just off Kirkwood, Soma is in close proximity to campus, making the location very convenient.
