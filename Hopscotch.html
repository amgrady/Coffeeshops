Hopscotch

Hopscotch’s black coffee/cold brews are reasonable priced, and well-flavored; however, when you start to dive into the speciality drinks, the selection lowers and the prices rise. They have a few syrups to choose from, but you won’t find much variety. The price is high for the quantity given. If you like old-fashioned coffee, like americanos and cappuccinos, this is the place for you!

Specialty Drink- “Cardamom Latte”
This drink is lightly sweetened and has a strong coffee taste. The cardamom syrup is more savory than sweet, but not overwhelming. It’s a very authentic latte; if you like drinks that taste like milk and sugar where the coffee flavor is hard to detect, this isn’t the drink for you.

Atmosphere:
The atmosphere of Hopscotch has a good balance between study and hangout; there are couches for a more relaxed, social experience, and tables with chairs for customers more focused on work or studying. It is often busy, but it does have lots of outdoor seating, which makes it a perfect spot to be in nice weather. It’s right on the B-line as well, making the location ideal to walk or bike to.
